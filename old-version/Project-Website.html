Axolotl Rescue

Together with a group from university, I developed an educational game designed to illustrate fundamental concepts of computer science. It covers topics such as binary numbers, circuits, encryption, breadth- and depth-first search, and shortest path algorithms—all woven into an engaging and story-driven experience.

Albion Guide

My journey into web development began with a passion project: creating a dedicated website for the MMORPG Albion Online. The idea was to design useful tools for the community, including a market tracker, trading utilities, an island calculator, and strategy tips. What started as a simple concept quickly grew into a project full of possibilities, sparking countless ideas I’m excited to explore.
