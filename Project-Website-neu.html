Project collection

These are some of my projects from the last few years. I started with editing, then moved on to 3D animations, and more recently to programming. Among them are larger projects, such as the ATN animation and the Axolotl Game, which took several months to complete. You’ll also find a few smaller ones here that I really enjoyed creating—like this website, which also happens to be my very first web development project.

Project collection website - 09.25.

This project marked my first step into building web tools and provided a solid introduction to HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It gave me the foundation to continue experimenting, and now I’m able to showcase a range of additional projects created with these technologies.

"Axolotl Rescue" lerning game - 07.25.

Together with a group from university, I developed an educational game designed to illustrate fundamental concepts of computer science. It covers topics such as binary numbers, circuits, encryption, breadth- and depth-first search, and shortest path algorithms all woven into an engaging and story driven experience.

"Dresden Half" route animation - 07.23.

In this project, I was able to fully explore my creative freedom by producing a route animation for the 2023 Dresden Half marathon. It was a great opportunity to combine design and storytelling in a visually engaging way.

"ATN" advertising animation- 05.23.

The ATN promotional animation was one of my larger projects, spanning several months. I created multiple complex animations to clearly explain mechanical processes and bring the concept to life.

"Game guide" website - W.I.P

My journey into web development began with a passion project: creating a dedicated website for the MMORPG Albion Online. The idea is to design useful tools for the community, including a market tracker, trading utilities, an island calculator, and strategy tips. What started as a simple concept quickly grew into a project full of possibilities, sparking countless ideas.

"Progress Station" trailer - 09.25.

A group of friends released an IDLE game and introduced a major update. I had the opportunity to edit a dynamic and engaging trailer that highlighted the new features and helped present the game to the community.

"NSS MIC" live stream - 11.23.

I supported the Nuclear Science Symposium and Medical Imaging Conference in Vancouver, Canada, by providing remote assistance. From my location, I helped manage operations and troubleshoot issues from afar.

"MID.SUMMER.TRACK.NIGHT" trailer- 07.23.

The MIT.SUMMER.TRACK.NIGHT in Dresden was my first independent project, where I handled everything from filming and music selection to video editing.
